HEALTH is an American noise rock band formed in Los Angeles in 2006. The band's lineup consists of Jake Duzsik, Jupiter Keyes, John Famiglietti, and Benjamin Jared Miller. Known for their experimental and abrasive sound, HEALTH has carved a unique niche in the alternative music scene.

The band gained critical acclaim for their self-titled debut album, released in 2007, which featured the popular track "Crimewave." HEALTH has since continued to evolve their sound, incorporating elements of noise, electronic, and industrial music into their discography.

With their intense and energetic live performances, HEALTH has built a dedicated fanbase. Their albums, including "Get Color" and "Death Magic," showcase a dynamic range of sonic landscapes, blending distorted guitars with electronic beats and haunting vocals.
