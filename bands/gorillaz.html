Gorillaz is a virtual band created in 1998 by musician Damon Albarn and artist Jamie Hewlett. The band consists of four animated members: 2D, Murdoc Niccals, Noodle, and Russel Hobbs. Each member has a unique personality and backstory, contributing to the band's fictional universe.

Known for their eclectic musical style that incorporates elements of rock, hip-hop, electronic, and alternative genres, Gorillaz has achieved global success with hit songs like "Clint Eastwood," "Feel Good Inc.," and "Demon Days."

Over the years, Gorillaz has collaborated with a diverse range of artists, creating a rich discography that resonates with fans worldwide. With their innovative blend of music, animation, and storytelling, Gorillaz continues to push boundaries in the music industry.
