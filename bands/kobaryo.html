Kobaryo, also known as KOBARYO, is a Japanese electronic music producer and DJ. Known for their energetic and fast-paced tracks, Kobaryo has gained recognition in the hardcore and electronic dance music (EDM) scenes.

Kobaryo's music often features a fusion of hardcore, breakbeat, and various electronic genres, creating a unique and dynamic sound. With a focus on high BPM (beats per minute) and intricate compositions, Kobaryo's tracks are celebrated for their intensity and creativity.

As a prolific artist, Kobaryo has released numerous albums, EPs, and singles, contributing to the vibrant and diverse landscape of the electronic music community. Their innovative approach to production and performance has garnered a dedicated fanbase around the world.
